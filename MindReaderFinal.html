Grab a pencil and paper.


Write down a random four digit number like 9485 or 3456...


Step 1 of 4

Now scramble your number, so if you had 9485 you can make 4859


Step 2 of 4

Now subtract the smaller number from the larger number, like 9485 - 4859 = 4626


Step 3 of 4

Circle a digit from your new number, except zero because it's already a circle.
